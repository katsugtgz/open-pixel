# Open Pixel Context

## Glossary

**Cozy Resource-Village Loop**:
The gameplay identity where players do calm resource actions, help a small village, and see immediate visible progress. The loop is about readable effort, collection, restoration, and guided objectives, not combat, speculation, or marketplace activity.

**Mechanic Grammar**:
The reusable interaction language of a game: how players recognize actionable objects, choose tools, receive feedback, track progress, and turn effort into rewards.

**Layout Composition**:
The specific spatial arrangement, silhouettes, colors, prop placement, and screen framing of another game scene.

**Layout Grammar**:
The high-level spatial pattern that makes a play space understandable: where objectives tend to sit, how paths guide movement, how resource areas are grouped, and how the player reads a scene at a glance.

## Relationships

- **Open Pixel** may borrow **Mechanic Grammar** from cozy pixel resource games.
- **Open Pixel** may borrow **Layout Grammar** from cozy pixel resource games.
- **Open Pixel** must not copy exact **Layout Composition** from Pixels or any other specific game.
- **Cozy Resource-Village Loop** depends on visible object states, immediate feedback, and lightweight objectives.
